Hi, I am Jaime, a qualified and experienced Therapeutic Massage Therapist at Essenciel.

I am Lindsay Cowley from Canada. I am a Registered Holistic Nutritionist and Wellness Coach available in Essenciel.

About Holistic Nutrition I look at the person as a whole, taking into account food, lifestyle, emotional and spiritual aspects that can affect your overall wellness. Each person is unique, therefore, there is no ‘one size fits all’ approach to a clients well being.

Although most people are aware of the benefits of sound nutrition, the range of conflicting information available to the consumer is often confusing. I do not advise you on a food guide or pyramid, but on what is best for you as an individual at the time.I guides my clients through the overwhelming maze of information from books, magazines, supplements and diets on the market. I work with clients to identify and help correct the nutritional causes of diseases, and they are qualified to design personalized food and lifestyle programs that optimize health.

About Lindsay I have a holistic approach to helping you reach your wellness goals – it’s not only about what you eat, it’s about healthy lifestyle too! My approach is not about quick fixes, magic pills or fad diets so, if that’s what you’re after, you’ve found the wrong person.On the other hand, if you are feeling motivated and ready to make change, you have found a great fit! I am all about long-term sustainable change, so you feel good today and tomorrow too.

I will be your guide to vibrant health by helping you find delicious, nourishing and beautiful food. I believe it’s the little things that can make a big difference. Sustainable change means baby steps and I will be by your side every step of the way. I am your partner in healthy living.

“Give your soul some space to grow.”
