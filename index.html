Our Philosophy

Essenciel is an Organic and Natural Health centre base in Wellington on Cuba Street

We aim to provide the best therapeutic treatments to suit your own needs as we all are unique human beings.

We will work with you to create a plan for regular, ongoing massage and therapy sessions that can keep you healthy while working with your lifestyle, your health concerns and your priorities.

In Essenciel we are sustainable, eco-friendly and we only use Natural and Certified Organic Products,we believe we need to take care of ourselves,and our Mother Earth.
